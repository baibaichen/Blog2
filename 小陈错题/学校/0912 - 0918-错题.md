    证明：  
    $$
    \begin{aligned}
    \dfrac{a+c}{b+c} - \dfrac{a}{b} &= b(a+c) - a(b+c)\\

\[
\begin{aligned}
&\frac{a}{\sqrt{b}} + \frac{b}{\sqrt{a}} - (\sqrt{a} + \sqrt{b})\\
&= \frac{a\sqrt{b}+b\sqrt{b}-a\sqrt{b}-b\sqrt{a}}{\sqrt{ab}}\\
&= \frac{(\sqrt{a}-\sqrt{b})(a-\sqrt{ab}+b)}{\sqrt{ab}}\\
& \geq 0
\end{aligned}
\]

\[
\frac{a}{\sqrt{b}} + \frac{b}{\sqrt{a}} \geq \sqrt{a} + \sqrt{b}.
